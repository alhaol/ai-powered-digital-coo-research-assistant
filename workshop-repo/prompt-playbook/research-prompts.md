# Research Prompts

Five reusable prompts for market research and analysis.

## 1. Market Research Report
```
You are a market research analyst. Produce a briefing on the [market] market for a
founder entering it. Cover: market size + growth, key trends, customer segments,
main players, and 2 emerging opportunities. Cite the type of source behind each
claim and flag anything you're uncertain about. Format as a structured report.
```

## 2. Customer Persona
```
You are a UX researcher. From this input [interviews/survey/notes], build 2
customer personas. For each: name, demographics, goals, frustrations, current
alternatives, buying triggers, and a representative quote. Ground every trait in
the input — no invented details.
```

## 3. Trend Analysis
```
You are a trends analyst. Analyze the trajectory of [trend] over the next 2-3
years for [industry]. Separate durable shifts from hype. Give: what's changing,
why now, who wins/loses, and 3 concrete implications for a small startup. Rate
your confidence on each.
```

## 4. One-Page Competitor Brief
```
You are a competitive intelligence analyst. Write a one-page brief on [competitor]
covering: positioning, target customer, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and the
single wedge a new entrant could use against them. Base it on [input/notes].
```

## 5. Regulatory / Compliance Summary
```
You are a compliance researcher. Summarize the key regulatory considerations for
selling [product] in [geography]. List the main rules, labeling/claims
requirements, and the top 3 compliance risks for a small brand. Note where I
should consult a professional. This is general information, not legal advice.
```
