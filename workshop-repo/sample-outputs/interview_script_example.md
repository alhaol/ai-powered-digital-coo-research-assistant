# Example Output — Exercise 1B: Customer Interview Script

## GlowEarth Customer Discovery Script (10 Questions)

*Rule: ask about their life and past behavior — never pitch the product.*

### Block 1 — Warm-up (build context)
1. **"Walk me through the last time you bought a skincare product. What made you
   pick that one?"**
   *Learning: real decision criteria, not stated ideals.*
2. **"How do you currently decide whether a brand is actually 'sustainable'?"**
   *Learning: do they have a method, or do they give up?*

### Block 2 — Pain-point discovery
3. **"Tell me about a time you felt misled by a 'clean' or 'green' beauty claim."**
   *Learning: emotional intensity of the greenwashing problem.*
4. **"When you can't verify a claim, what do you do?"**
   *Learning: current coping behavior and its cost.*
5. **"How much time do you spend researching products before buying?"**
   *Learning: the cost of the problem in time.*

### Block 3 — Current alternatives
6. **"Which brands do you buy now, and what frustrates you about them?"**
   *Learning: switching triggers and unmet needs.*
7. **"Have you ever tried to look up where a product's ingredients come from?"**
   *Learning: demand for traceability, revealed not stated.*
8. **"What would have to be true for you to switch brands?"**
   *Learning: the real bar for adoption.*

### Block 4 — Willingness-to-pay signals
9. **"What did you pay for your current skincare, and how did you decide that was
   worth it?"**
   *Learning: price anchor and value justification.*
10. **"When was the last time you paid extra for a product because you trusted the
    company more? What was it?"**
    *Learning: proven willingness to pay a trust premium.*

> **Facilitator tip:** After each interview, write down the *exact words* the
> person used. Those verbatims become your landing page copy.
