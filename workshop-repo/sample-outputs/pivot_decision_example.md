pre-order conversion on the QR-traceability landing page. If conversion is < 3%
after $200 of ad spend, transparency is a "nice to have," not a buying trigger.

**5. Confidence Level:** HIGH

hypothesis lowers price risk from an unsupported 20% to a validated 10-15%.
