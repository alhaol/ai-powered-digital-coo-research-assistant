# SOP: Customer Refund Request Processing
**Version:** v1.0 · **Owner:** Founder (Priya) · **Last reviewed:** 2026-07-15

## 1. Purpose & Scope
Standardize how GlowEarth handles refund requests so every customer gets a fast,
fair, consistent response. **Covers:** product refunds within 30 days of delivery.
**Does not cover:** wholesale/B2B orders or chargebacks (see Finance SOP).

## 2. Step-by-Step Process
1. **Log** the request in the support tracker within 24h of receipt.
2. **Verify** order number, delivery date, and reason.
3. **Classify** the reason: (a) damaged/defective, (b) reaction/dissatisfaction,
   (c) changed mind / didn't open.
4. **Decide** refund type using the decision rules below.
5. **Communicate** using the matching email template.
6. **Process** the refund in the payment processor; **close** the ticket.

## 3. Decision Points
- **If** product arrived damaged/defective → **then** full refund, no return
  required; log a QA note.
- **If** opened + dissatisfaction/reaction → **then** full refund; request a photo
  for QA; no return required (hygiene product).
- **If** unopened + changed mind, within 30 days → **then** full refund on return.
- **If** > 30 days OR clear misuse → **then** offer 50% store credit; escalate if
  the customer disputes.

## 4. Email Templates

**Acknowledgment**
> Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out — we're sorry [product] wasn't right. We've
> logged your request (#[ID]) and will have a resolution within 1 business day.

**Approval**
> Hi [Name], your refund of $[amount] is approved and processed. It'll appear in
> 5-10 business days. We'd love to hear what went wrong so we can do better.

**Denial / Alternative**
> Hi [Name], because [reason], we can't issue a full refund, but we'd like to make
> it right with [50% store credit / replacement]. Just reply and we'll set it up.

## 5. Escalation Criteria
Escalate to the founder if: refund > $150, a customer threatens a chargeback or
public complaint, or a suspected quality/safety issue across multiple orders.

## 6. KPIs to Track
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Refund rate (% of orders) | < 5% |
| First-response time | < 24h |
| Resolution time | < 3 days |
| Post-refund CSAT | > 4.0 / 5 |
